FAQs

Common Questions Answered

How does the rental model work exactly?

You pay a fixed monthly or annual fee. We supply the equipment, install it professionally, and cover all maintenance throughout the rental period. No upfront investment, no surprise repair bills.

Can I rent just one service, like only CCTV?

Yes. You can rent any one service line independently — CCTV, UPS/inverter, or EPABX. You don't need to take a bundle, though bundles offer better value.

What areas do you service in Delhi NCR?

We cover Delhi (all districts), Noida, Greater Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. If you're unsure, call us and we'll confirm availability for your area.

What brands of CCTV and UPS do you install?

We work with leading brands including Hikvision, Dahua, CP Plus for CCTV; APC, Luminous, Microtek, Su-Kam for UPS; and Panasonic, Matrix, Beetel, Grandstream for EPABX.

What does an AMC contract include?

AMC contracts include scheduled preventive maintenance visits, emergency breakdown support, labour for repairs, and minor consumables. Major parts may be charged separately depending on the contract tier.

How quickly can you respond to a breakdown?

Response times depend on location and contract type. AMC and rental clients get priority response. We aim for same-day or next-day on-site response across NCR.

Can you expand or upgrade an existing system?

Yes. We can add cameras to existing CCTV systems, extend EPABX with more lines and extensions, and upgrade UPS batteries or capacity — for all brands, not just systems we installed.

Is there a minimum rental period?

Rental plans typically start at a 12-month term. We can discuss shorter arrangements depending on the service and equipment involved. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
